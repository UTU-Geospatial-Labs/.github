Welcome to [UTU Geospatial Labs'](https://geospatial.utu.fi/) GitHub page! Explore publicly shared educational materials and research-derived resources from the Department of Geography and Geology at the University of Turku.

# About us
We are operational at the University of Turku, and our mission is to provide and facilitate geospatial resources for research and education. This GitHub page is used to share educational materials used on courses at the Department of Geography and Geology, as well as code, data and processes developed by research teams.
