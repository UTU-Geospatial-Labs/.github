Welcome to [UTU Geospatial Labs'](https://geospatial.utu.fi/) GitHub page! Explore publicly shared educational materials and research-derived resources from our team.

# About us
We are operational at the University of Turku and our mission is to provide and facilitate expertise and geospatial resources for research and education. Our team is engaged in activities that apply digital geospatial data, technologies and tools for societal impact.

This GitHub page is used to share educational materials used on courses at the Department of Geography and Geology, as well as code, data and processes developed by research teams.

# Education

## MAAN7771 Geospatial data management and visualisation
This Master's level course provides practical skills in geospatial data management, including data aquisition, maintenance, updating, quality assessment, data sharing and visualization.
